# Fungi National Institute of Science and Technology - Brazil

## About INCT-Fungos

The **Fungi National Institute of Science and Technology (INCT-Fungos)** is a Brazilian research network dedicated to advancing the study of fungi and their applications in science, medicine, agriculture, and biotechnology.

### Mission

INCT-Fungos promotes collaborative research focused on:

- **Biodiversity**: Exploring and cataloging fungal diversity across Brazilian biomes
- **Taxonomy & Systematics**: Developing comprehensive taxonomic frameworks for fungal species
- **Genomics & Molecular Biology**: Investigating fungal genetics and molecular mechanisms
- **Biotechnology**: Discovering novel compounds and applications from fungi
- **Medical Mycology**: Understanding fungal pathogens and developing therapeutic solutions
- **Agricultural Applications**: Studying fungi's role in sustainable agriculture and biocontrol

### Network

INCT-Fungos brings together leading researchers, institutions, and laboratories across Brazil, fostering international collaborations to advance mycological research and training the next generation of scientists in the field.
